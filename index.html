About

This Is What we Do:

W3Develops

Email: w3develops@gmail.com

Have you ever wanted to just pair up with other programmer's and learn coding by building real world applications? We take motivated people and put them together with other like minded individuals in a community where they have the opportunity to level-up their skills and ideas by building to learn. To join a cohort(team) click the "Apply" linked above, then about 2 weeks before the new cohort start date (1st of every month), we will send the applicants their invites. Feel free to join if you are serious and focused on learning to code. If you have any questions or ideas, let's chat (feel free to e-mail us!).

W3Develops Process

Follow these steps:

  • Step 1

    APPLY

    To learn to build projects in a team environment and beef up your portfolio to get a developer job.

  • Step 2

    JOIN A TEAM

    Commit yourself by joining a team of like-minded developers.

  • Step 3

    BUILD PROJECTS

    Work among other developers and put your knowledge to the test.

  • Step 4

    GO TO MEETUPS

    Join or host local and online meetups.


  • SUCCEED!
